[bzoj 3516] 国王饮水记加强版

Descrption:
求∑i=1nmiim\sum_{i=1}^{n}{m^ii^m}∑i=1n​miim

Solution:
设f(i)=∑k=1nkiimf(i)=\sum_{k=1}^{n}{k^ii^m}f(i)=∑k=1n​kiim
运用扰动法得出

(m−1)f(i)=∑k=1nki∗mk+1−∑k=1nki∗mk(m-1)f(i)=\sum_{k=1}^{n}{k^i*m^{k+1}}-\sum_{k=1}^{n}{k^i*m^k} (m−1)f(i)=k=1∑n​ki∗mk+1−k=1∑n​ki∗mk

	 $$=n^im^{n+1}+\sum_{k=1}^{n}{m^k\sum_{j=0}^{i-1}{C(i,j)(-1)^{i-j}k^j}}$$
	 $$=n^i+m^{n+1}+\sum_{j=0}^{i-1}{C(i,j)(-1)^{i-j}f(j)}$$

O(m2)O(m^2)O(m2)递推即可。
